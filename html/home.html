Random Questions

  1. Are there certain parts of letters which are more/less important to legibility? If so, it would be interesting to see the effects of teaching kids to write while emphasizing those parts of each letter (so those dotted lines we used to practice on maybe are a bit bolded in the important parts). It'd be cool if this could improve legibility.
  2. Currently, American high school math is extremely application focused, which leads to a very skewed perception of what math is really like. There definitely are quite a bit of benefits of teaching application focused math (namely that most people don't pursue math as a career), but I wonder if it would be more beneficial for everyone if proof-based mathematics was introduced earlier. Would application based math classes benefit from this kind of rigor for common people, or would it just be a distraction?
